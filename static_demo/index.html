    Golden Hour

    Call ambulance and police first. Share exact location, number of victims, visible injuries, and vehicle hazards.

    Scene Safety

    Park away from traffic, switch on hazard lights, warn vehicles, avoid fuel leaks, and do not crowd the victim.

    CPR

    If trained, begin chest compressions in the center of the chest after calling emergency services.

    Bleeding

    Apply firm pressure with a clean cloth. Keep the injured area raised if possible.

    Burns

    Cool under clean running water for 20 minutes. Do not use ice, oil, or toothpaste.

    Fractures

    Keep the person still, support the injured area, and do not force the bone back.
